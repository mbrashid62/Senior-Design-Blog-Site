Lab 2 is completed!

Posted by Bill on November 17th, 2015

Although it wasn't easy, we finished. As a group we did OK however, we didn't quite tap into our full potential this time around.

Our group seems to work best when we're all on the same page and working as a team. I believe we did only a mediocre job of doing that for this lab which explains our mediocre result.

For the future, we will focus on implementing more team-oriented approach. I believe a big part of this will be dependent on our ability to determine how each member can apply their talents to help the group. We did a great job of this for lab one but for some reason fell short this time.

Because it is important not to get too hung up on not getting the result we want, we are using this as a learning experience. For next time, we know what we have to do in order to be succesful which will help us for not only Lab 3, but further down the road as well.
